The highest power of the variable in an equation is known as degree of the equation.

Identification of Quadratic Equation

A Quadratic Equation is the second order polynomial in single variable.

It has two solutions.

Solutions may be both Real or both Complex.

Example:

ax2+bx+c=0

a,b and c are constants
